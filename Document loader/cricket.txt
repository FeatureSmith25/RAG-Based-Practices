The pitch is rolled, the skies are bright,
The fields are packed with green and white.
The bowler runs, the leather flies,
Beneath the glowing summer skies.

A sudden crack, the willow swings,
Across the grass the boundary rings.
The batsmen run to seal the score,
As cheering crowds cry out for more.

The wickets fall, the game is done,
Shared memories of sport and sun.
With handshakes given, side by side,
They leave the field with team-won pride.
